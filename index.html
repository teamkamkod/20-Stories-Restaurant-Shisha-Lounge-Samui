Welcome to 20 Stories

Beachside Seafood Bliss & Shisha Serenity in Bo Put.

Our Menu Book a Table

Savor fresh grilled seafood platters under Samui stars.

Nestled on Bo Put's vibrant beach in Koh Samui, 20 Stories Restaurant & Shisha Lounge Samui fuses the ocean's bounty with exotic hookah allure.

  • Fresh grilled platters: lobster, snapper, octopus, mussels, and prawns.
  • Beachfront tables with mesmerizing sea views and fire shows.
  • Relax with chilled rosé, cocktails, and shisha under the stars.

We cherish fresh ingredients, value, and unforgettable vibes—creating stories worth sharing. Join us for lunch or dinner, daily from 2 PM.
